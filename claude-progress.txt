### Key Finding: `max_files_changed` is the dominant behavioral parameter
Tight caps (3) force 1-feature-per-loop. Relaxed caps (5) enable scope creep.
The arena contract alone cannot enforce discipline — the cap is the enforcement mechanism.

Same bmark baseline + tight caps (files=3/2) + Arena Contract addition:
"Read EVALS/ledger.jsonl to see previous verdict before planning" → agent can learn
from discard_audit even after PROGRESS.md rollback.
